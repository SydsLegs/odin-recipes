Chicken Alfredo

Description

This is my description of the recipe. Its Chicken Alfredo, what do you want from me

Ingredients

  • 8oz noodles
  • 2 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1/4th cup Kraft italian dressing
  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1.66 cups milk
  • 4oz cream cheese
  • 1/4th cup Kraft parm
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil (ew, use real basil)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta as directed on package, adding broccoli to the water for the last 2 minutes. Drain.
  2. While that is cooking, heat dressing in large skillet. add chicken and cook until 165 degrees (about 6 minutes)
  3. stir milk, cream cheese, parm, and basil into chicken pan until well combined, about 2 minutes
  4. add chicken mixture to pasta mixture and stir
