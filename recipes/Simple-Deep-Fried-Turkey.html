Simple Deep Fried Turkey

Description

This is my description of the recipe. Its a deep fried turkey, what do you want from me

Ingredients

  • 3.5 gallons peanut oil
  • 1 (10 Pound) whole turkey, neck and giblets removed
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 Tbsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil to 350 on stove.
  2. Trim turkey up so any extra skin or loose bits are removed
  3. Pat the bird dry and season with salt and pepper
  4. Gently lower turkey into pot
  5. Cook turkey for 3.5 minutes per pound (about 35 minutes for a 10 pound bird)
  6. Remove turkey from pot and let rest for at least 15 minutes before slicing
