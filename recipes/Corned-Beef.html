Corned Beef

Ingredients

  • 1 (5.5lb) corned beef brisket with spice packet
  • 7 small potatoes
  • 4 medium carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 C)
  2. Place beef in the center of roasting pan. Arrange potatoes and carrots around the sides and scatter onion and garlic on top. Add seasoning packen then cover with water until potatoes are almost covered
  3. Cover with foil and place into the oven
  4. Roast for about 5 hours or until fork tender
